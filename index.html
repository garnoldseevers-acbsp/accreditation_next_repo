Advance your ACBSP Accreditation

Institutions and organizations that achieved ACBSP accreditation have a proven dedication to excellence. The journey towards next level performance excellence can continue through Accreditation Next, a collaboration of ACBSP and The Baldrige Alliance for Performance Excellence.

ACBSP members can pursue three tiers of Accreditation Next; Accreditation Plus, Platinum and Diamond. The three tiers qualify organizations for excellence recognition by Baldrige Alliance programs. Diamond tier recipients are eligible to apply to the national Baldrige program and compete for the Malcolm Baldrige Nationa

Accreditation Next focuses on process and results:

  • Continue the journey towards performance excellence by becoming a "role model" program
  • Enhance the benefits of accreditation based on global principles for quality higher education, such as CHEA
  • Align with world-class frameworks used by role model organizations — Baldrige, EFQM, Japan Quality Award, China Quality Award, Tata Business Excellence Group, and others
  • Avoid the unnecessary costs of additional programmatic accreditation by embracing business relevant performance standards
  • Gain real world organizational experience to manage change in performance excellence
"ACBSP is unique among business school accreditation associations in its 30-year relationship and explicit use of the Baldrige Framework for Performance Excellence as the foundation of its accreditation standards and criteria and review process." — The Baldrige Alliance for Performance Excellence

Eligibility

ACBSP accredited members are eligible to apply. An application committee will review the processes already in place at the school and recommend the level at which each applicant school will apply. Some institutions may be further along in their business program practices and could enter the award program at the Platinum or Diamond Level, rather than beginning at Accreditation Plus.
